Design Philosophy

A Masterpiece of
Italian Artistry

The Superveloce captures the spirit of the most beautiful MR. VIKING bikes of the past, incorporating all the modern technology that has made MR. VIKING legendary. Every curve, every line, every surface is designed to evoke emotion and deliver uncompromising performance.

The distinctive round headlight, the elegant tail section, and the hand-painted livery pay homage to the golden era of Grand Prix racing while housing state-of-the-art electronics and engineering.
